Several resources are available to students and faculty for creating, storing, and sharing files with others.
  • Google Drive may be used for collaborative projects and storing many files
    • Accessed by visiting https://drive.google.com and signing into your University Gmail account.
    • Files may be created, edited, and shared online.
    • Allows multiple people to simultaneously edit a file, if necessary.
  • UVa Box is available and provides 1TB, approximately 1000GB, of storage to each student and faculty member.
    • Files may be created and shared through Box.
    • After leaving UVa, users may continue to store up to 50GB through Box.
