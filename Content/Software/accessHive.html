Some software not available through UVA Software may be accessed on the Hive. This service allows you to use the software virtually without installing it on your PC. You may use it at any time, but there may be limitations on the number of licenses available, so it is recommended you use it as early as possible whenever it is actually needed. Software that may be found on the Hive includes:
  • Stata
  • ArcGIS
  • SAS
  • SPSS
