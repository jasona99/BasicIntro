1. Connect to the “Welcome_to_UVa_Wireless” network.
  2. Launching a web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari and attempting to visit any website, such as virginia.edu, will redirect to the Getting Connected page. Click continue under "Faculty, Staff, and Students"
  3. Download the Network Setup Tool for your computer, open it, and follow on-screen instructions.
  4. Allowing the tool to add the certificate to your browsers will allow you to sign into NetBadge just by clicking “Log In” under the digital certificate option.
