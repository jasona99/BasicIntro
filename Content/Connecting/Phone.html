This will vary based on whether you have an Apple device, Android device, or some other device.
For Apple devices:
  1. Connect to "Welcome_to_UVa_Wireless"
  2. Launch Safari, click "Continue" under "Faculty, Staff, and Students"
  3. Run the tool under the iOS device option.
  4. You should now be connected to the secure "cavalier" network

For Android or other devices:
  1. Connect to "Welcome_to_UVa_Wireless"
  2. Launch a browser of choice, click "Continue" under "Faculty, Staff, and Students"
  3. Click "Manually configure and register device" and follow the provided instructions
  4. You should now be connected to the hidden "wahoo" network
If there is a need to connect an Android device to the secure network, contact the Batten Helpdesk.
