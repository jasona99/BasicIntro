In some cases, it may be necessary to download the digital certificate. This certificate should never be given to anyone else and should not be stored on public computers.
Visit https://standard.pki.virginia.edu/pkcs12/ and follow the given instructions after reading and agreeing to the policy.
If able to obtain the certificate with the provided information, it will be downloaded to your computer. The password for it will be your permanent NetBadge password.
This may be used for configuring browsers not supported by the Network Setup Tool to identify yourself to NetBadge or for connecting certain devices to the “cavalier” network.
